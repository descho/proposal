What is an Industry Research Project

The IRP (Industry Research Project) is the culmination of a Hyper Island Masters programme. We are expected to do research, formulate hypotheses, test them, and present a work-based project that adds value and challenges the industry. The output of the project can be a final report or a creative portfolio, equivalent to between 10 and 15,000 words. Elements of the portfolio should include work done with clients/within businesses.

As part of their IRPs previous Hyper Island students have worked with large design firms like IDEO and AKQA, social-good focused companies like Impact Hub, and student-created organisations like The Pop-Up Agency.
